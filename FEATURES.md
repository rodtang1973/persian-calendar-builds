# Persian Calendar Features

This document provides a detailed overview of the features available in the Persian Calendar app.

## Calendar

The Calendar is the main feature of the app, providing a comprehensive view of dates and events. It is designed to be user-friendly and customizable, allowing you to tailor it to your needs.

### Main View

The main view of the calendar displays a full monthly calendar, with the current date highlighted for easy reference. You can navigate between months by swiping left or right, and you can quickly return to the current month by tapping the "Today" button.

### Multiple Calendar Systems

The app supports multiple calendar systems, including Persian, Gregorian, and Islamic calendars. You can switch between these calendar systems to view dates in your preferred format. The app also allows you to see the equivalent date in other calendar systems, making it easy to coordinate with people who use different calendars.

### Event Management

You can easily add, view, and manage events directly from the calendar. To add an event, simply tap on the desired date and enter the event details. You can also set reminders for your events, so you never miss an important appointment.

### Year View

In addition to the monthly view, the app also provides a year view, which allows you to see the entire year at a glance. This is useful for long-term planning and getting an overview of the year's events.

### Navigation

The app provides several ways to navigate through the calendar:

-   **Swipe Gestures:** You can swipe left or right to move between months.
-   **"Today" Button:** Tapping the "Today" button will instantly take you back to the current month.
-   **Date Picker:** You can use the date picker to jump to a specific date, month, or year.

### Customization

The calendar is highly customizable, with options to change the theme, show or hide week numbers, and select which calendar systems to display. You can access these options in the app's settings.

## Athan/Prayer Times

The Persian Calendar app includes a feature to display prayer times (Athan) and provide notifications for them. This feature is designed to be highly customizable to suit your needs.

### Prayer Times Display

The app calculates and displays the daily prayer times for your location. You can view the prayer times for the current day, as well as for any other day, by navigating through the calendar.

### Athan Notifications

You can enable notifications for each prayer time. When a prayer time arrives, the app will play the Athan (call to prayer) to remind you. You can customize the Athan sound and set a custom volume for the notification.

### Qibla Compass

The app includes a Qibla compass that helps you find the direction of the Kaaba in Mecca. This is essential for Muslims who need to know the direction of prayer.

### Customization

The Athan/Prayer Times feature is highly customizable:

-   **Location:** You can set your location manually or use the device's GPS to get accurate prayer times for your specific area.
-   **Athan Sound:** You can choose from a variety of Athan sounds or even use your own custom sound file.
-   **Volume:** You can set a custom volume for the Athan notification, so it doesn't interfere with your other activities.
-   **DND (Do Not Disturb):** The app is designed to respect your device's Do Not Disturb settings, so you won't be interrupted during important moments.

### Sun, Moon, and Planets

### Solstices and Equinoxes

The app shows the dates and times of the solstices and equinoxes, which mark the changing of the seasons. This is useful for those who follow seasonal events and celebrations.

### Zodiac Information

You can view detailed information about the zodiac signs, including their symbols, ruling planets, and associated dates. The app provides information for both the tropical and sidereal zodiac systems.

### Moon Phases

### Planetary Hours

### Customization

The astronomy features are highly customizable:

-   **Time and Date:** You can view astronomical data for any date and time, allowing you to explore past and future celestial events.

## Compass/Qibla

The Persian Calendar app includes a compass feature that not only shows the direction of North but also helps you find the Qibla, the direction of the Kaaba in Mecca.

### Qibla Direction

The compass displays a clear indicator for the Qibla direction, making it easy for Muslims to find the correct direction for prayer. The app uses your location to accurately calculate the Qibla direction.

### Sun and Moon Positions

In addition to the Qibla, the compass also shows the real-time positions of the sun and moon in the sky. This can be useful for various purposes, such as photography, astronomy, and outdoor activities.

### True North and Magnetic North

The app allows you to switch between true north and magnetic north, depending on your needs. True north is the direction to the geographic North Pole, while magnetic north is the direction that a compass needle points to.

### Calibration

To ensure accuracy, the app provides guidance on how to calibrate your device's compass. This helps to minimize interference from magnetic fields and provides a more reliable reading.

### Customization

-   **Show/Hide Qibla:** You can choose to show or hide the Qibla indicator on the compass.
-   **True North:** You can switch between true north and magnetic north in the settings.

## Level

The Persian Calendar app includes a handy level tool that helps you determine if a surface is horizontal (level) or vertical (plumb). It also includes a ruler for taking measurements.

### Bubble Level

The app provides a bubble level that works just like a traditional spirit level. It displays a bubble that moves to indicate the angle of the surface. When the bubble is centered, the surface is level.

### Surface Level

In addition to the bubble level, the app also provides a surface level that shows the orientation of your device in two dimensions. This is useful for leveling surfaces in both the horizontal and vertical planes.

### Ruler

The app includes a ruler that can be used to measure lengths in both centimeters and inches. You can switch between the two units of measurement with a simple tap.

### Full-Screen Mode

For a more immersive experience, you can switch to full-screen mode, which hides the app's interface and displays only the level tool. This is useful when you need to focus on the task at hand.

### Customization

-   **Units:** You can switch between centimeters and inches for the ruler.
-   **Calibration:** While the app is designed to be accurate, you can calibrate the level tool to ensure it is perfectly aligned with your device.

## Map

The Persian Calendar app includes a map feature that provides a visual representation of the world, with a focus on astronomical and location-based information.

### Day/Night Cycle

The map displays the day/night cycle, showing which parts of the world are currently in daylight and which are in darkness. You can scrub through time to see how the day/night cycle changes throughout the day and year.

### Qibla Direction

The map shows the direction of the Qibla (the direction of the Kaaba in Mecca) from any point on the globe. This is a useful tool for Muslims who need to find the correct direction for prayer, especially when traveling.

### Sun and Moon Positions

The map displays the real-time positions of the sun and moon, showing you where they are currently located in the sky. This is a great feature for astronomers, photographers, and anyone interested in celestial events.

### Location Information

You can tap on any point on the map to get detailed information about that location, including its coordinates, time zone, and current time.

### Customization

-   **Map Type:** You can switch between different map types, including a simple map, a satellite map, and a crescent visibility map.
-   **Grid:** You can choose to show or hide a grid on the map, which can be useful for navigation and estimating distances.
-   **Location:** You can choose to show or hide your current location on the map.

## Date Converter

The Persian Calendar app includes a powerful date converter that allows you to convert dates between different calendar systems and perform various calculations.

### Calendar Systems

The app supports a wide range of calendar systems, including:

-   **Persian (Jalali):** The official calendar of Iran and Afghanistan.
-   **Gregorian:** The most widely used calendar in the world.
-   **Islamic (Hijri):** The calendar used by Muslims to determine the proper days on which to observe Islamic holidays and rituals.

You can easily switch between these calendar systems to view and convert dates.

### Date Conversion

To convert a date, simply select the date in one calendar system, and the app will instantly show you the equivalent date in the other supported calendar systems. This is useful for planning events with people who use different calendars or for historical research.

### Distance Between Dates

The app can calculate the number of days, months, and years between two dates. This is a handy tool for calculating age, planning long-term projects, or simply satisfying your curiosity.

### Time Zone Converter

The app includes a time zone converter that allows you to see the time in different parts of the world. You can add multiple time zones to your list and see the current time in each of them at a glance.

### Other Tools

In addition to date and time conversion, the app also includes a calculator and a QR code generator.
